HOPE



As the owner of AA Campsite, Mrs. Evelyn Lijunin hopes that with the establishment of eco-tourism areas such as AA Campsite, it will be able to help the surrounding residents by providing employment opportunities to them, helping to contribute in the eco-tourism sector in the Papar district. Mrs. Evelyn also hopes that with the opening of such an area can bring the name of Papar district, especially Kg. Bolotikon Papar, Sabah to be known to the public who have never known about eco-tourism places such as the only AA Campsite.
