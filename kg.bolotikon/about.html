HISTORY AND CULTURE KG. BOLOTIKON PAPAR, SABAH.



Kg. Bolotikon Papar, Sabah is one of the villages in the district in Sabah. The majority of the residents in this village are Kadazandusun. The origin of the name of the village Bolotikon is taken in conjunction with the name of a tool used by the villagers in the past to catch wild boar. The tool is known as "Balatik" which means snare or trap. The villagers at that time found that many of their plants were destroyed as a result of the disturbance of the wild boar. With the "Balatik", the residents can catch the wild boar from roaming and indirectly saved their plants from being damaged. What is interesting in the village of Bolotikon Papar, Sabah, there are three eco-tourism areas and one of them is AA Campsite Kg. Bolotikon Papar, Sabah.



HISTORY ABOUT AA CAMPSITE KG.BOLOTIKON PAPAR, SABAH.



AA Campsite Kg. Bolotikon Papar, Sabah is one of the eco-tourism areas in Kg. Bolotikon. AA Campsite Kg. Bolotikon Papar, Sabah was officially opened to the public in November, 2019. AA Campsite was opened after the owner of this eco-tourism area, Mrs. Evelyn Lijunin, received support and encouragement from her friends who saw that the area has the potential for visits from public and the establishment of this AA Campsite is based on a family business. The name for AA Campsite is inspired by the children of Mrs. Evelyn Lijunin, namely Anastasia and Ashley. In addition, this short name is taken to make it easier for visitors to remember the name of the place. The attractions that can be seen at AA Campsite are such as the river, hanging bridge, Mini Nursery (Kebun Mama), and the village atmosphere that tourists can feel for themselves how life in the village because in this area there are rubber trees that allow tourists to feel for themselves methods of tapping rubber.
